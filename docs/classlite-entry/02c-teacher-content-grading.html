Two-sided opinion essay — technology in education

Student · Duc Tran Submitted Apr 5 · on time 287 / 250 words Submission 8 of 14
Some people believe that technology in education has improved learning outcomes, while others argue that it has reduced students' ability to think independently. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
✎ ! ★ ⌖
Comment mode 287 words

In recent years, the role of technology in education has become a topic of considerable debate1. While proponents argue that digital tools have transformed how students learn, critics contend that over-reliance on devices2 may diminish independent thinking. This essay will examine both perspectives before offering a personal view.

On one hand, technology offers unprecedented access3 to information and learning resources. Students can now research any topic instantly, watch educational videos from world-class universities, and collaborate with peers across the globe. Furthermore, this also personalised learning platforms4 adapt to individual pace, helping struggling students catch up while challenging advanced ones.

On the other hand, critics warn that constant use of devices undermines the patience required for deep, sustained thought. When answers are always one search away, students may lose the habit of working problems through for themselves.
