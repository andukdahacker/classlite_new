Chapter 7 · screens 68 – 73 Owner

Money & limits — when the product meets the wallet

Six chapters in, the product has been free. This chapter draws the seam where it isn't: where the plan picker lives, how Huong sees what she's paying for and against what limits, what invoices look like and what happens when a charge bounces, and the upgrade flow she lands in when she hits a wall. Billing is Owner-only — Admins see billing-adjacent signals in their Inbox (S52) but never the Settings > Billing page itself, Teachers and Students never see this surface at all. The whole chapter exists to make plan boundaries feel negotiable, not punitive — every limit-hit moment offers a path forward instead of a dead end.

71 / Owner · Upgrade modal

The contextual upgrade modal. Same modal triggers from three places: the Plan picker (S68) "Upgrade to Studio" button, the Class-creation form (S65) when the student cap is exceeded, and the Billing dashboard (S69) usage meters. Shows price delta with the prorated charge math made visible, what changes (limits, features), and a single confirmation. Modal is shown over a faintly-dimmed billing dashboard so the context is preserved.

The People > Students page when one class is at 18 of 20 students. Jan 15 renewal charge was declined (insufficient funds / fraud block). Today is Jan 18, day 3 of the 7-day grace window. The center is still fully functional, but a red strip runs across the top of every Owner-facing page counting down to downgrade. The Billing dashboard explains what failed, what happens at each milestone (day 3 reminder email, day 7 auto-downgrade to Free), and the recovery path: update the payment method, then retry.
